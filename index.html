Image Classifier for Kitesurfing

Tracking cameras are getting very popular. One example is the Soloshot, which is a camera, gimball, and tracking software system that follows a sports player. A similar solution is follow me mode on a drone in which the drone will track a moving person. I've been thinking about building a tracking camera for Kitesurfers. As a first step I built an image classifier for kites.

Convolutional Neural Networks for Classifiying CIFAR-10 Images

My results on fitting the CIFAR-10 dataset with a image classifier.

Blogging for MapQuest

As a paid blogger for MapQuest, I wrote a series of posts illustrating how to use the MapQuest Javascript API for annotating maps.

Space Robotics Challenge Vision Pipepline

As a team member competing in the Space Robotic Challenege I wrote an iPython notebook used to locate a sequence of flashing LED's. The next steps are to implement the transformations from images to the real world location of the LEDs.
