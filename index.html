As part of a team competing in the Space Robotic Challenege I wrote an iPython notebook for the computer vision portion of Task #1. In Task #1 stereo cameras are used to locate a sequence of flashing LED's. The notebook shows how to locate the LED in imgaes from the Gazebo Simulation. The next steps are to implement the calcualation in ROS and implement the image coordinate to 3D real world coordinate transformations. more...
